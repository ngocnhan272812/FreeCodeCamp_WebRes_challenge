Summary of Uncle Ho's revolutionary activities

  • 1890 - 1911: Childhood and Youth of President Ho Chi Minh
  • 1911 – 1920: President Ho Chi Minh accepted Marxism-Leninism and affirmed the path of the Vietnamese Revolution
  • 1920 - 1924: President Ho Chi Minh struggled to protect and creatively apply the line of V.I Lenin on national and colonial issues
  • 1924 – 1930: President Ho Chi Minh founded the political party of the Vietnamese working class
  • 1930 – 1945: President Ho Chi Minh - Organized and led the victorious August Revolution and founded the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
  • 1946 – 1954: President Ho Chi Minh, Organizer and leader of the struggle to maintain the revolutionary government and resistance against the French colonialists
  • 1954 – 1969: President Ho Chi Minh led the Socialist Revolution in the North and fought against American aggression, liberated the South and reunified the country.

At 9:47 a.m. on September 2, 1969, his heart stopped beating, leaving infinite grief for the entire Vietnamese people and international friends.
